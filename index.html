I am a graduate student in Compute Science at New York University (NYU). Before coming to NYU, I was an Artificial Intelligence (AI) engineer at Internet companies in China. My working experiences include Senior Algorithm at Meituan-Dianping, Algorithm Expert at Alibaba, and Senior AI engineer at PayPal. I got my Master degree in Computer Science from Fudan University, advised by Professor Xuanjing Huang and Professor Qi Zhang. My research interests lie in the broad field of artificial intelligence and machine learning, particularly focus on natural language processing.

My long term goal is to help computers perceive languages like humans and to break down barriers in human-machine communication. I would like to work together with wonderful researchers at NYU.
